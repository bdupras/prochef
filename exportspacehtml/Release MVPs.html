Opscode is quite proud of the Chef community


We usually give a Most Valuable Player (MVP) award for each release to individuals from the community who contributed greatly to that release.


Hall of Fame

These contributors are dependable leaders in the Chef community and earned themselves three MVP awards. This enters them into the hall of fame with our deepest gratitude.

  • Matthew Kent
  • Doug MacEachern
  • Tollef Fog Heen
  • Thom May
  • Bryan McLellan
