Mailing Lists

All members of the Opscode community can always post to the Chef Mailing Lists for interaction and assistance.

Joining the Chef and/or Chef-Dev Mailing Lists can be an excellent way to hear and participate in discussion on Chef use, gain tips and information on use of Chef, and to provide input to product futures.

Sign up for the Chef Mailing Lists at http://lists.opscode.com.
