I'm organizing a Chef meetup group, how do I get started?

  • Subscribe to the Community Organizers listserv.
  • Email community@opscode.com and let us know who you are, where your group is, when you are planning your next event.
  • Tag your meetup group with "Opscode" so it appears at http://opscode.meetup.com

What support is available for for Chef Community Meetup Groups?

We want to do everything we can to help support the growing list of meetups.

  • Opscode will:
    • Reimburse up to $200 per event of pizza/food. (Recipts will be required to make sure tax issues don't come up.
    • Sponsor or reimburse the fee for your meetup group.
    • Send tshirts & stickers for you to distribute
    • Promotion of Meetups to our various lists and channels.
    • Make sure you have regular appearances from interesting Opscode folks, if you want that.
    • Give you discount codes for training etc.
